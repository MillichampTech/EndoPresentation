DIAGNOSIS

  • Suspected Endometriosis in Women
  • (including young women aged 17 and under)
  • presenting with 1 or more of the following symptoms and signs:
  • Chronic pelvic pain
  • Dysmenorrhoea
  • Deep pain during or after Sex
  • GI symptoms
  • Urinary symptoms
  • Infertility
